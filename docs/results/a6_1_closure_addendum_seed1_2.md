## Decision

Freeze A6/A6.1 as a useful schema and analyzer scaffold, not as evidence for
attractor-like dynamics, lobe grammar, synchrony, causal support, or nonlinear
collective structure.

The A6.1 pilot/null gate produced conservative closure for the current
single-hive smoke route: logistic readiness and artifact-utility advantages do
not survive the preregistered source-preserving nulls on paired seeds `1` and
`2`, and residual rows remain underdetermined at smoke scale.

Do not reopen A6.1 with more seeds, new mechanisms, or multi-hive coupling from
the seed `1..2` result alone.

## Evidence Boundary

The A6 analysis gate in `docs/results/a6_analysis_gate_seed1_2.md` confirmed
that the read-only analyzer emits paired control deltas, residual preflights,
residual timeseries, residual contrast rollups, comparison/effects consistency
checks, artifact-provenance rows, and source-accounting rows.

That gate did not promote A6. Logistic-minus-linear artifact utility was tiny,
seed signs disagreed, mean queue depth was higher for logistic, mean completion
fraction was lower, and all residual rows were labeled
`underdetermined_smoke_scale`.

The A6.1 source-accounting audit in
`docs/results/a6_1_source_accounting_audit_seed1_2.md` confirmed that fresh
source-schema artifacts expose the required source fields and reconstruct
artifact deltas. That audit also retained the conservative interpretation:
logistic readiness and utility rows kept large handoff-success shares, and
sixteen rows were labeled `high_handoff_alias_risk`.

The A6.1 pilot/null gate in
`docs/results/a6_1_pilot_null_gate_seed1_2.md` then added the preregistered
source-label-shuffled and handoff-success-timing-broken null artifacts. All
eight pilot gate rows were labeled `null_removes_endpoint_advantage`. The nulls
matched or exceeded the logistic readiness/utility endpoints, while
backlog-adjusted productivity did not rescue the result.

## Stop Conditions

Do not add more A6.1 mechanics, broader seed sweeps, dashboards, external
integrations, real LLM calls, Lean, Slack, browser automation, Atomspace
integration, or downstream multi-hive coupling from this result.

Acceptable reasons to revisit the A6/A6.1 artifact set are limited to:

- a concrete simulator or analyzer bug that changes the recorded result;
- a narrow read-only validation over existing artifacts;
- a new preregistered A6.2 residual-recurrence design written before any
  implementation.

## Future Boundary

Any future A6.2 reopening must start with a preregistration, not code. It must
define the scientific claim, source-accounted residual recurrence endpoints,
required controls, source-preserving nulls, backlog-adjusted productivity
guardrails, paired seed streams, and closure/promotion rules before
implementation.

The default next scientific move is therefore planning, not simulation: draft a
new A6.2 residual-recurrence preregistration only if the project chooses to
reopen single-hive A6 after this conservative closure.
